Cold Coffee

A chilled drink to refresh your mood instantly.

Ingredients

  • 1 glass milk
  • 1 tsp coffee
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add milk, coffee, sugar, and ice into a blender
  2. Blend until frothy
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy cold

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
