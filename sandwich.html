Veg Sandwich

A simple and filling snack made in minutes.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Chopped vegetables
  • Sauces and Spices

Steps

  1. Spread butter on bread slices
  2. Add vegetables, sauces, and spices
  3. Grill or toast the sandwich

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
