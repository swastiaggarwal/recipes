Pani Puri

A popular street food loved for its spicy and tangy taste.

Ingredients

  • Puri Balls
  • Boiled potatoes and Chanas
  • Spicy and Sweet pani

Steps

  1. Fill puris with mashed potatoes and chana
  2. Add flavored pani inside
  3. Serve immediately for best taste

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
