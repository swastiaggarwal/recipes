White Sauce Pasta

Creamy and delicious pasta for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • spices

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until soft
  2. Prepare white sauce using butter, flour, and milk
  3. Mix pasta with sauce, add spices and cook briefly

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
