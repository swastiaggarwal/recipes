Masala Maggi

A quick and comforting snack perfect for late-night hunger.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • Maggi tastemaker

Steps

  1. Heat water in a pan until it starts boiling
  2. Add the noodles and let them soften
  3. Mix in the tastemaker
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
